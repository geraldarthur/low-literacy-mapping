Adult Low Literacy Rates

An experimentation in making a responsive D3 map, and cleaning up data with TopoJSON.

This map was created as a prototype map for a WAMU 88.5 story. I took bulky 2000 Census shapefiles and created lightweight JSON files. Although the clients wound up going with Mapbox in the end (desire for pan, zoom and geolocation features), here's a finalized version of the D3 map.
